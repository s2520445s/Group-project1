What is League Esports?

It's the professional competition of League of Legends, made by Riot Games!

It's one of the largest Esports in the world, with one of the biggest prizes!

Over 4,000 professional players, play against each-other to bring home the trophy!

There are 8 different pro leagues with their own regions and players as well!

The best of the best play on the big stage in front of millions of people!
